Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We typically respond to live chat within minutes, email within 2-4 hours, and phone calls immediately during business hours.

What information should I include in my support request?

Please include your account details, a clear description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and any relevant screenshots or error messages.

Can I get help with content creation strategies?

Yes! Our support team includes content strategy specialists who can help you optimize your content and grow your audience.

Is there a limit to how many support requests I can submit?

No limits! We encourage you to reach out whenever you need help. Premium subscribers get priority support.
