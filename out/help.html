How do I create my first post?

getting-started

Creating your first post is easy! Click the "Create Post" button, write your content, add media if desired, choose your privacy settings, and hit post. You can also schedule posts for later.

What file formats are supported for media?

content-creation

We support images (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP) up to 10MB and videos (MP4, MOV, AVI) up to 100MB. For audio, we support MP3, WAV, and AAC formats up to 25MB.

Why can't I upload my video?

troubleshooting

Check that your video is under 100MB and in a supported format (MP4, MOV, AVI). Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, try uploading during off-peak hours.
